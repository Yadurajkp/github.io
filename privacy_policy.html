ProMeet — Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 28, 2025

Welcome to ProMeet. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and your rights. By using ProMeet, you agree to the collection and use described below.

1. Information We Collect

  • User-provided data: username, profile picture, meeting titles and descriptions, and messages you post in meetings.
  • Device & usage data: device model, OS version, app version, IP address, crash logs, analytics events (feature usage, pages visited).
  • Media: any photos or files you upload to share in meetings (stored only if you choose to upload).

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide and maintain the app features (meetings, messaging, notifications).
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs (crash reports, logs).
  • Analytics to understand feature usage and improve the product.
  • To comply with legal obligations or to protect rights and safety.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We don't sell personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, push notifications) under contract to help operate the app.
  • Law enforcement or regulators when required by law.

4. Storage & Security

We store data on hosted servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, but no service can be 100% secure. If you notice suspicious activity contact us immediately.

5. Your Choices

  • You can update your profile and settings inside the app.
  • To request deletion of your account and data, use the Delete Account page or contact promeetpolicy@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

ProMeet is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, contact us to request removal.

7. International Transfers

Data may be stored and processed outside your country. By using the app you consent to transfer of data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We’ll post the new policy here with a new effective date.

Contact: For questions about this policy, email promeetpolicy@gmail.com.
